Toyota Mark 2 JZX90

A classic from Toyota

Description

The Toyota Mark 2 JZX90 is a mid-size sedan produced by Toyota from 1992 to 1996. It is known for its sleek design, powerful engines, and exceptional handling.

Features:

  • Engine Options: 1JZ-GTE, 2JZ-GE
  • Transmission: 4-speed automatic or 5-speed manual
  • Drive Type: Rear-wheel drive
  • Suspension: MacPherson strut front suspension and multi-link rear suspension

Special Features

  • Engine Options: The Toyota Mark 2 JZX90 was offered with a range of engine options, including the powerful 1JZ-GTE and 2JZ-GE engines known for their performance and reliability.
  • Performance: With its rear-wheel-drive layout and responsive handling, the Mark 2 JZX90 provided an exhilarating driving experience with quick acceleration and precise control.
  • Luxurious Interior: Featuring comfortable seating, high-quality materials, and modern amenities, the Mark 2 JZX90 offered a luxurious and refined interior environment.
  • Versatility: Available in sedan and wagon configurations, the Mark 2 JZX90 provided versatility to suit different needs, whether for daily commuting or family trips.
  • Aftermarket Support: Enthusiasts appreciate the strong aftermarket support for the Mark 2 JZX90, with a wide range of aftermarket parts and accessories available for customization and performance upgrades.
  • Timeless Design: Characterized by its sleek and understated design, the Mark 2 JZX90 has a timeless appeal that continues to attract attention and admiration.
  • Reliability: Built with Toyota's renowned reliability and durability, the Mark 2 JZX90 is known for its longevity and dependability, making it a trusted choice for enthusiasts.

Toyota

Toyota Motor Corporation is a Japanese multinational automotive manufacturer headquartered in Toyota City, Japan. Founded in 1937 by Kiichiro Toyoda, the company has grown to become one of the largest automakers in the world.

Toyota is known for its commitment to quality, innovation, and sustainability.

Kiichiro Toyoda

Kiichiro Toyoda was a Japanese industrialist and the founder of Toyota Motor Corporation. Born on June 11, 1894, in Shizuoka Prefecture, Japan, he was the son of Sakichi Toyoda, the founder of the Toyoda Group. Kiichiro succeeded his father as head of the Toyoda Group in 1930.

One of Kiichiro Toyoda's most significant contributions was his decision to venture into automobile manufacturing. He recognized the potential for growth in the automotive industry and established the Toyota Motor Corporation in 1937.

Under Kiichiro's leadership, Toyota developed innovative production methods such as the Toyota Production System, which revolutionized the automotive manufacturing process. His vision and dedication laid the foundation for Toyota's success and its reputation for quality and efficiency.
